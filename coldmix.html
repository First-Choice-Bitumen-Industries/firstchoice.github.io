ColdMix

ColdMix Bitumen is a ready-to-use, cold-applied bituminous mixture designed for quick and effective road repairs and maintenance. It eliminates the need for heating, making it safer and more convenient for patching potholes and minor roadwork.

What is ColdMix?
ColdMix is produced by blending bitumen emulsion with selected aggregates at ambient temperature. The mixture remains workable at lower temperatures and can be applied directly from the bag or container, providing a fast and efficient solution for road maintenance.

Why ColdMix?

  • Easy and safe to use—no heating required
  • Quick application and traffic-ready within hours
  • Works in all weather conditions, including wet or cold environments

ColdMix Bitumen is ideal for pothole repairs, patching, edge repairs, and small-scale surfacing, offering a cost-effective and durable alternative to traditional hot mix asphalt.

Applications

  • Pothole and patch repairs
  • Edge and shoulder repairs
  • Temporary surfacing and utility reinstatements

Frequently Asked Questions

What is ColdMix Bitumen?
ColdMix Bitumen is a pre-mixed, cold-applied asphalt mixture used for road repairs and maintenance without the need for heating.
What are the benefits of using ColdMix?
ColdMix is easy to use, requires no heating, can be applied in any weather, and allows roads to be reopened to traffic quickly after repairs.
Is ColdMix durable?
Yes, ColdMix provides a strong and flexible repair that can withstand traffic loads and adverse weather conditions.
Where can ColdMix be used?
ColdMix is suitable for pothole repairs, patching, edge repairs, and temporary surfacing on highways, city roads, and rural roads.
