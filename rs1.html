RS-1 (Rapid Setting) Emulsion

RS-1 is a high-performance rapid setting cationic bitumen emulsion utilizing modern emulsion chemistry for quick, reliable, and green road construction solutions.

What is RS-1?
RS-1 is manufactured by blending carefully selected bitumen, water, and eco-safe emulsifiers in precise proportions. The result is a stable liquid that spreads thinly and bonds instantly upon application to aggregates or old road surfaces.

Why RS-1?

  • Delivers extremely fast set/break for minimal project downtime
  • Creates a tenacious, flexible tack layer between asphalt layers
  • Excellent penetration and adhesion, even on slightly damp or dusty bases
  • Complies with leading Indian and international standards (IS 8887, MoRTH)
  • Eco-friendly, ready-to-use, safer than hot bitumen, lower VOCs and energy usage
  • Speeds up site turnover: Most roads can reopen to traffic quicker

This makes RS-1 the contractor’s choice for national highway works, rural roads, urban patch repairs, and any project where time, safety, and bond strength are critical.

Applications

  • Tack coat on existing bituminous or concrete pavements before laying new asphalt layers
  • Patchwork and pothole repair for highways, city roads, and rural streets
  • Surface dressing and preventive road maintenance
  • Primer on granular base or sub-base (where specified)

Frequently Asked Questions

What is RS-1?
RS-1 is a rapid-setting, cationic bitumen emulsion used primarily as a tack coat in road construction projects.
What is the coverage per drum?
Approximately 80–100 m² per 200kg drum (as tack coat), depending on surface texture and porosity.
Is dilution with water allowed?
Dilution is recommended only as per project specifications. Excessive dilution can affect performance.
How do I store RS-1 emulsion?
Keep drums tightly sealed, protected from direct sunlight and freezing conditions.
