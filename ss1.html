SS-1 (Slow Setting) Emulsion

SS-1 is a slow-setting cationic bitumen emulsionallowing extended work and mixing times for thorough blending with fine aggregates or dust. It ensures uniform priming and stabilization for strong, lasting road foundations.

What is SS-1?
SS-1 is manufactured by carefully blending high-quality bitumen, water, and environmentally friendly emulsifiers in precise proportions to produce a stable emulsion. This formulation offers extended workability, allowing it to spread uniformly and penetrate deeply, ensuring superior bonding with aggregates and existing road surfaces.

Why SS-1?

  • Extended workability and break time
  • Great adhesion and bonding properties
  • Acts as a moisture barrier

This makes SS-1 the contractor’s choice for prime coats, soil stabilization, gravel roads, and applications where extended workability, uniform penetration, and strong moisture resistance are essential.

Applications

  • Prime coats on untreated surfaces
  • Soil stabilization in road bases
  • Dust control on unpaved roads

Frequently Asked Questions

What is SS-1?
SS-1 is a slow-setting, cationic bitumen emulsion designed for applications requiring extended mixing and work time, such as prime coats, soil stabilization, and dust control.
What is the coverage per drum?
Coverage is typically 80–100 m² per 200kg drum for prime coat applications, but may vary based on surface texture and porosity.
Can SS-1 be diluted with water?
SS-1 can be diluted with water if required by project specifications. Always follow recommended dilution rates to maintain emulsion performance.
How should SS-1 emulsion be stored?
Store SS-1 in tightly sealed drums, away from direct sunlight and freezing temperatures. Mix gently before use if stored for extended periods.
