MS (Medium Setting) Emulsion

MS stands for Medium Setting Cationic Bitumen Emulsion, engineered for applications that require a balance between workability and quick setting. MS emulsions are ideal for premix applications, patching, and surface dressing where moderate mixing time and rapid strength development are needed.

What is MS?
MS is produced by blending premium bitumen, water, and specialized emulsifiers to create a stable emulsion with medium setting characteristics. This allows enough time for mixing with aggregates while ensuring the emulsion breaks and sets quickly after application, providing strong adhesion and durability.

Why MS?

  • Balanced workability and setting time
  • Excellent adhesion to aggregates
  • Suitable for patching and surface dressing

MS emulsion is the preferred choice for cold mix, patch repair, and surface treatments where both workable mixing time and rapid strength gain are essential for efficient road maintenance and construction.

Applications

  • Cold mix for patching and repairs
  • Surface dressing and maintenance
  • Premix for road construction

Frequently Asked Questions

What is MS emulsion?
MS is a medium setting cationic bitumen emulsion designed for applications like cold mix, patching, and surface dressing, offering a balance between workability and quick setting.
What is the coverage per drum?
Coverage depends on the application and aggregate type, but typically ranges from 80–100 m² per 200kg drum for surface dressing. Actual coverage may vary based on surface conditions.
Can MS emulsion be mixed with aggregates?
Yes, MS emulsion is specifically formulated for mixing with aggregates in cold mix and patching applications, providing good coating and adhesion.
How should MS emulsion be stored?
Store MS emulsion in tightly sealed drums, away from direct sunlight and freezing temperatures. Stir gently before use if stored for extended periods.
